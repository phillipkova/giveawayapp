Privacy Policy

Sparkify (referred to in this policy as “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects information from you when you subscribe to our newsletter, respond to our survey, and join our giveaways. You may be asked to enter your name, e-mail address, and/or any other information that is relevant. You may, however, visit our site anonymously.

We respect your privacy. We are committed to ensuring your personal information is kept safe and confidential so rest assured that we will not transfer your personal information to third parties for any reason, except if ordered to do so by a law enforcement, or government agency.

Like many site operators, we also use cookies and web beacons to collect information that your browser sends whenever you visit our site. This information may include data such as your computer’s Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type and version, pages in our site that you visit, time and date of your visit, time spent on the site, and other statistics. This information are not linked to your personal identifiable information and are only used for analytics purposes.

We also use third party advertisements to support us financially. Some of these advertisers may also use cookies and web beacons to collect information. This information is generally used for geotargeting purposes or showing certain ads based on specific sites you visited.

You can opt to disable or selectively turn off our cookies or third-party cookies in your browser settings.

Information collected while joining giveaways coordinated by us will be saved only for purposes of picking winners and to notify an entrant has won the giveaway. Any physical addresses collected for purposes of the giveaway will only be used only to send prizes and will not be saved or used for any other purpose.

This privacy policy was last modified on May 18, 2018.

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page. If you have any concerns please send us a message through our contact page.
